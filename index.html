About me!

My name is Sean Gray and I’m a junior dev. My former career was as a musician working in events, sometimes as an artist liaison / stage hand, sometimes as a sound guy helping to rig venues. I recently decided to give that up though in pursuit of a more meaningful career.

Software development landed on my radar two years ago, I remember doing free courses on codeAcademy on the basics of HTML and CSS, now I’ve built my own APIs and find myself looking at videos on machine learning and AI algorithms.

I want to be able to combine both my past and my present, to be able to create high functioning apps with flair and creativity, and genuinely build programs that will end up helping people.
